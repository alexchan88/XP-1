Your Action Items

The closure of this DRMS Engagement Workspace is the final step in your electronic audit documentation completion. In order to appropriately stop the 45-day audit documentation completion period clock:

  • Create workpaper binders for any hard copy audit workpapers and other documentation.

  • Print this email and the KARDs work paper delivery ticket that was received by the Engagement Manager at your report release date.

  • Print out and complete the eAudIT Record Retention Checklist.

    • For files completed in eAudIT on DCSS or Workgroup/Workgroup+ the checklist can be accessed by clicking here.

    • For files completed in eAudIT on Server 2, the checklist can be accessed by clicking here.

  • Take the following to Records Center Services for filing and recording receipt of retention files into the KARDs system:

    • eAudIT Record Retention Checklist

    • any hard copy workpaper binders

    • the printed KARDs workpaper delivery ticket

    • the DRMS Workspace Closure email

Note: If there is no hard copy audit documentation, this email, the eAudIT Record Retention Checklist and the KARDs work paper delivery ticket are still required to be delivered to Records Center Services.
