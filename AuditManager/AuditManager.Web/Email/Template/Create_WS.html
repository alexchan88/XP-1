Additional Team Access

Click here to access the Audit DRMS Manager page to grant additional engagement team members access to upload records to and/or view records within this DRMS Engagement Workspace.

Note: Engagement team members who are granted administrative access will have the ability to complete all actions within the workspace including acknowledging retention files and requesting re-processing of rollforward or retention files.

Upload Final Documentation

Click here to upload your final documentation (i.e. records) to this DRMS Engagement Workspace.

Note: It is no longer necessary for files larger than 2GB to be stored on the large file retention server. All files can now be uploaded directly within DRMS Manager. To expedite the upload process, it is recommended to perform this from a KPMG office.

Removal of Accidental Uploads

In the event a file that is not considered final documentation or other documentation is accidentally uploaded to the DRMS Engagement Workspace, it needs to be removed unless the engagement is subject to a preservation notice.

If the document has not been marked as a record of the firm through closing the workspace, identify the file(s) to be removed, select Remove in the “Action” drop down menu.

If the document has been marked as a record of the firm, click here to send an email to the DRM Governance Office to request removal of the file(s). The email should include a complete list of file(s) to be removed and should include the approval of the Engagement Partner listed in SYNC.

Closure and Archiving of Records

After successfully uploading all of your electronic documentation into the DRMS Engagement Workspace, a team member with administrative or read/write level access may initiate closure of the DRMS Engagement Workspace.

Note: The team member initiating closure of the workspace should be careful to mark all uploaded files that should be marked as records when initiating closure.
